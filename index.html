History

FRX Crew, established in August 2018, stands as the official dance group of the College of Information and Communications Technology (CICT) at Bulacan State University, Malolos, Bulacan.
As a college-based organization operating under the Office of Student Affairs (OSOA), we've become an integral part of the university's vibrant cultural tapestry.

Beyond the dance floor, FRX Crew has become a close-knit family—a supportive network where members forge lifelong friendships and share a passion for the transformative power of dance. We continue to evolve, inspire, and leave an indelible mark on the cultural landscape of Bulacan State University.
